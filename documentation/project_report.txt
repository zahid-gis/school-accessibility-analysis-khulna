# School Accessibility Analysis in Khulna District

## Introduction

Access to educational facilities plays an important role in improving educational opportunities. GIS-based accessibility analysis helps identify well-served and underserved areas.

---

## Study Area

Khulna District, Bangladesh.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze school accessibility.
- Measure accessibility coverage.
- Calculate accessible road network.
- Identify underserved areas.

---

## Data

OpenStreetMap

Geofabrik Bangladesh Extract

---

## Methodology

1. Data Collection
2. Boundary Extraction
3. School Extraction
4. Buffer Analysis
5. Dissolve
6. Road Clipping
7. Area Calculation
8. Statistics
9. Map Design

---

## Results

Total Schools: 960

District Area: 4648.74 km²

School Buffer Area: 529.90 km²

Accessibility Coverage: 11.40%

Accessible Road Length: 2030.16 km

---

## Conclusion

The analysis shows that approximately 11.4% of the district lies within 500 meters of a mapped school. Areas outside this buffer may benefit from improved access to educational facilities. This project demonstrates the use of GIS techniques for spatial accessibility analysis and educational planning.
